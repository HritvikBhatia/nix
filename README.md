# Nix - AI-Powered Career Assistant

Nix is a modern web application designed to help job seekers prepare for technical roles. By leveraging Google's Gemini AI, Nix provides real-time resume evaluation (ATS scoring) and conducts interactive technical interviews with voice-to-text capabilities.

## üöÄ Features

* **ATS Resume Evaluation**: Analyze your resume against specific job categories to receive an ATS score, a summary of strengths, and actionable improvement points.
* **AI Technical Interview**: Participate in a structured, 10-question technical interview tailored to your profile (skills, projects, and education).
* **Voice-to-Text Support**: Answer interview questions using your microphone for a more realistic experience.
* **Comprehensive Evaluation**: Get a detailed breakdown of your interview performance, including scores per question, comparisons with ideal answers, and an AI-generated performance summary.
* **State Persistence**: Your progress and evaluations are saved across sessions using Redux Persist.

## üìñ Usage

### 1. Dashboard

Start by uploading or entering your resume details (education, projects, and skills). This data is stored in the Redux state and used to personalize your experience.

### 2. ATS Score

Navigate to the **ATS Score** section, enter the job categories you are targeting (e.g., "Frontend Developer"), and let the AI evaluate your resume structure and keyword relevance.

### 3. Interview

* Click **Start Interview** to generate 10 technical questions (4 Easy, 4 Medium, 2 Hard).
* You can type your answers or use the **Microphone** icon to dictate them.
* After submitting, the AI will grade your responses based on accuracy and clarity.

### 4. Evaluation

Review your **Overall Score** and a detailed **Breakdown**. You can expand each question to see how your answer compares to the "Ideal Answer" generated by the AI.
